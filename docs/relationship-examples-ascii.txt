KEY INSIGHT
===========

The nodes are just labels. The RELATIONSHIPS contain:
  * WHY things happened
  * HOW I learned
  * WHAT mistakes I made
  * WHEN to use which approach
  * HOW CONFIDENT I should be
  * WHAT evidence supports claims
  * HOW understanding evolved

This is where the real knowledge lives!
